ozonia® Expands Installations in China
Jul 9, 2004, 13:41

Caption describing this image
Jiaxing, China - ozonia® Ltd. (Zurich) has been selected by the City of Jiaxing for the supply of a 30 kg/h (1,584 lb./day) ozone system as part of an overall upgrade to the Shijiuyang Water Treatment Plant. The Shijiuyang WTP selected the ozonia® system as part of an effort to reduce their chlorination use by at least one half. The existing plant will be modified to use pre-ozonation before coagulation and settling, followed by main ozonation and GAC filters before final chlorination. The Shijiuyang WTP serves approximately 300,000 people. The plant upgrade is a fast-track project scheduled for completion in early 2004.
