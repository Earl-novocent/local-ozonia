Applications
Swimming Pools
Aug 9, 2004, 09:11

Ozone and UV are very effective alternatives to chlorine and other chemicals as a disinfection agent in swimming pools.   Ozone and UV can safely prevent the formation of chlorine  byproducts, such as chloramines and trihalomethanes.

Ozone and UV can breakdown chlorinated byproducts, therefore reducing the typical problems associated with pools such as red eyes and respiration ailments.   Ozone will also oxidize oils and other contaminants in swimming pools and spa water into safer elements.
The use of ozone and UV can play an essential role in providing a clean and pleasant environment for bathers and pool staff.
