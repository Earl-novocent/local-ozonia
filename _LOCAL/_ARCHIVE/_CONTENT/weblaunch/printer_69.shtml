Containerized Ozone Plants (COP)
Dec 15, 2004, 06:54

In order to meet clients’ demands for turn-key and fully assembled standard solutions, ozonia® manufactures a broad range of large and small containerized systems complete with feed gas preparation, ozone production and ozone injection apparatus and an assortment of control features.   Depending upon ozone demand the highly successful range Containerized Ozone Plants (COP) incorporate either compact OZAT®CFS ozone generators, skid-mounted OZAT®CFV ozone generators or one of the larger Engineered Ozone systems. These stand-alone type plants are built to a very high industrial standard and are intended for all types of applications. The technical features of the units are exceptional:

  • Very compact dimensions
  • Integrated controls
  • Very simple installation
  • Oxygen or air feed gas
  • High ozone concentrations
  • Robust industrial quality
  • High reliability and safety
  • Stand alone design
