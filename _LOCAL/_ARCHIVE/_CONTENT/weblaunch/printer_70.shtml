Membrel® Electrolytic Ozone Generators
Dec 15, 2004, 08:07

Membrel® electrolytic ozone generators generate ozone from the water, in the water.  Clean, pure, trouble-free.  Eliminate outside contamination.

First introduced in the 1980’s, ozonia®’ Membrel electrolytic ozone generators have firmly established themselves as the preferred method of ultra-pure water (UPW) disinfection for the pharmaceutical, cosmetics and semiconductor industries.  Hundreds are installed and operated around the world, most on USP validated systems.  Reserved for high purity water systems, the MEMBREL® MKII system has been completely redesigned to take advantage of the latest in electrolytic and electronics technologies.

  • Simple and easy to use
  • Compact and self-contained for minimal footprint
  • No gas preparation equipment
  • No ionic contamination
  • Minimal dissolved oxygen
  • Eliminates pH and conductivity swings associated with corona discharge ozonation
  • Direct or remote controlled operation
