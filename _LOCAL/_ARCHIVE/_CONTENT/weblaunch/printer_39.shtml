OZAT CFS-7
Ozone and/or Ultra Violet (UV) disinfection can play important roles in the prevention and elimination of fish diseases in aquaculture systems.   Ozone, a powerful oxidizing agent disinfects and oxidises organic pollutants while increasing the water’s dissolved oxygen level.   The end result is exceptional water quality, which is a fundamental requirement for intensive fish farming. 

Ultra Violet light systems economically disinfect make-up water in both fresh and saltwater farms without additional chemicals or bromate formation. 

ozonia® CFV
Both ozone and UV systems are easy to adapt to large scale ponds and raceways as well as small holding tanks and nurseries.   Improved water quality leads to reduced mortality rates and an increased yield of a healthier product.  Other benefits include a reduction of chemical additives for water treatment or vaccines and antibiotics.
