Ozonia Model LAB 2A Ozone Generator
Dec 15, 2004, 06:47

The LAB2A Ozone Generator is a small air-cooled unit specifically designed for bench top use for laboratory or other testing purposes.  

The generator uses a high voltage/high frequency power supply and an air-cooled corona discharge module with a robust ceramic dielectric.  The control circuitry is designed with a unique "fold-back" short circuit protection and allows fully variable ozone output control.    Ozone output control is manual only.  The units can operate under negative or positive pressures up to 1 barg.

The LAB2A produces 4 grams ozone per hour from dry air, or up to 10 grams ozone per hour from dry oxygen.
