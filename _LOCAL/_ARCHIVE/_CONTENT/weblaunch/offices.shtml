The ozonia® Group

ozonia® is a group of companies in Switzerland, USA, Russia, South-Korea and Scotland with our holding copmany, ozonia® International, based in France. ozonia® also has representatives and distributors in many countries world-wide.

ozonia® has the clear mission to specialize and promote leading edge ozone generation and ultraviolet (UV) technology for industrial applications and protection of the environment.
