ozonia® Radial Diffuser
Dec 15, 2004, 08:12

To achieve high mass transfer of ozone into water where conventional diffusion apparatus would be impractical or ineffective, ozonia® manufactures the Radial Diffuser System.

The radial diffuser provides a stable "plug-flow" type of hydrodynamic pattern and can be designed for counter-current or for descending or ascending co-current service, and is to be used when a risk of clogging of porous diffusers exists, or when a bubble column with porous diffusers does not provide the hydrodynamics required for optimum reaction control.

This patented system is composed of 4 main elements:

  • 1 motive water pump
  • 1 specially adapted ozone gas / motive water mixing device
  • 1 ozonia® Radial Diffuser, which disperses the ozone gas over the whole bubble column
  • 1 compact reaction chamber, where the liquid is being ozonated

With no moving parts other than a pump, the ozonia® Radial Diffuser constitutes an excellent alternative where traditional bubble column with porous diffusers can not be used.  It is applicable for fast (decolouration) as well as slow kinetics (COD elimination).  It is also very flexible with respect to the required ozone dose and can be used with only a few ppm ozone (drinking water pre-ozonation), to more than 1000 ppm (leachate).
