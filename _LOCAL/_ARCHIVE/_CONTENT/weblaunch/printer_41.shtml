TOGC Ozone Generators
Jul 9, 2004, 15:45

In the 2 to 13 gram per hour ozone production range ozonia® offers two ozone generators with integrated oxygen feed and control systems all in a convenient “plug and play” package. The TOGC8X and TOGC13X both employ and oil-less compressor, oxygen concentrator, a high voltage/high frequency electronic power supply, ceramic dielectric ozone modules and all electrical and pneumatic controls. The control circuitry has been specifically designed to give “fold-back” short circuit protection and variable frequency output to control the ozone production rate. Housed in a rugged steel cabinet (wall mount) the ozone generators are designed for continuous operation in an industrial environment.
