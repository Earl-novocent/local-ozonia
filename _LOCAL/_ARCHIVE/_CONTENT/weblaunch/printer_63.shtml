Aquaray® H2O UV Disinfection System
Dec 15, 2004, 06:00

The Aquaray® H2O is an innovative new solution for the disinfection of waterborne pathogens in municipal drinking waters.  Utilizing medium pressure, high output UV lamps, this technology is operational in over 50 installations worldwide for the treatment of potable, process and ultra pure water. 
  • Sophisticated PLC control system with SCADA or Ethernet capability
  • Extremely Small Footprint
  • Low Head Loss
  • One UV Sensor per lamp
  • Certified by German DVGW test protocol
  • Low Maintenance/Operator Friendly
  • Electronic Ballasts Offer More Control and Better Energy Efficiency versus Magnetic Ballasts
  • Germicidally Selective Sensors Monitoring Each UV Lamp
  • Automated Cleaning System
