OZAT CFV Ozone Generators
Jul 9, 2004, 15:49

Ozonia’s CFV ozone generators are a perfect bridge between the small, economical CFS series and the more powerful CFL series, typically used in larger plants. The economical CFV series overcomes many price restrictions, thereby, making high quality ozone units available for more applications.

The new range of OZAT® compact ozone generators reflects years of experience and incorporates Ozonia’ latest technical developments. By using Ozonia' latest version of the Advanced Technology (AT) non-glass dielectric segments installed in a horizontal vessel, this new range incorporates an optimised medium frequency power supply which takes full advantage state of the art power electronic and semiconductor products. The CFV line has the same outstanding features as the other OZAT® ranges when it comes to economy with high industrial product quality. All of the units in the range are capable of producing ozone in concentrations up to 14 wt% from oxygen and 5 wt% from air.

Technical features of the OZAT CFV Ozone Generators units are exceptional:

  • Very compact dimensions
  • Integrated controls
  • Simple installation
  • High ozone concentrations
  • Robust industrial quality
  • High reliability and safety
  • Power factor = 0.99
  • Low harmonic content
