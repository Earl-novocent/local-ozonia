OZFIL Ozone Systems for Bottling Water
Dec 15, 2004, 06:41

Ozonia has been associated with the bottled water industry for decades and has consistently supplied the most reliable products and service available to for that market.  Continuing with our commitment to the bottled water market, Ozonia offers a complete stand alone ozone treatment system for the bottle filler.   Offered in nine sizes for flow rates from 15 m3/h to 70 m3/h (66 gpm to 310 gpm), OZFIL represents the most technically advanced yet economical ozone package on the market today.  Complete with a sophisticated control system to assure reliable and repeatable ozone measurement and control, each OZFIL system incorporates:

  • OZAT® CFS Ozone Generator
  • Air dryer system
  • Injector /contactor
  • Motive water pump
  • Contact chamber (316L SS)
  • Flow control monitor
  • Residual ozone analyzer
  • Vent ozone decomposer
  • PLC control system
  • Interconnections
  • Complete skid mounting and integration of the above
