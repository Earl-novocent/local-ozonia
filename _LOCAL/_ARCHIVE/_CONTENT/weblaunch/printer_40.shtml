Positive Pressure Ozone Generators

ozonia® offers two products in the 1 to 10 grams per hour range. The TOGC2 and LAB2B are both air-cooled employing a high voltage/high frequency power supply with “fold-back short circuit protection and ceramic corona discharge ozone dielectrics.

The TOGC2 ozone generator is a compact unit in a rugged 316 stainless steel enclosure for aggressive industrial applications. The unit is ideally suited for small food and beverage applications, car wash equipment, ozone pilot work and general small industrial systems. Designed for ambient air, dry air or oxygen feed gas.

ozonia®'s Lab 2B ozone generator is a small air-cooled unit specifically designed for bench top laboratory use. The LAB2B incorporates function indicators, a feed gas flowmeter and variable output control for maintaining precise ozone production. Designed for dry air or oxygen feed gas.
