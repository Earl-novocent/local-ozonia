ozonia®’s SMP Series UV systems
Dec 15, 2004, 06:25

ozonia®’s SMP series UV systems also use medium pressure UV lamps but are powered by magnetic ballasts with a tow step output variation of 100% and 50%.  The SMP series uses a greatly simplified control package intended for low flow applications, which do not require or justify the added cost of variable output control and SCADA communication capability.  UV intensity monitors and automatic wiping systems are standard.  Key features of ozonia®’s SMP series systems include:
  • Medium pressure UV lamps
  • Magnetic ballasts
  • Local or remote operation
  • Manual output
  • Programmable relay logic control
  • Two step UV output (100% and 50%)
  • UV intensity measurement and indication
  • Automatic wiping systems with adjustable wiping frequency
  • Polished 316L Stainless Steel reactors with 150# ANSI connections
