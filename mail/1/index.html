Open Channel UV Systems

The Aquaray® 3X and Aquaray® 40 HO high output ultraviolet systems effectively and efficiently deliver the degree of disinfection required for even the most stringent of effluent criteria, including wastewater reuse applications.

The Aquaray® 3X is designed for larger wastewater plants where energy consumption and small footprint are of the utmost importance, while the Aquaray® 40 HO is ideally suited to provide dependable disinfection for small to medium capacity wastewater plants.

 

Closed Channel UV System - Aquaray H20

The Aquaray H2O uses a powerful dose of UV light delivered by strategically placed medium pressure lamps to disinfect drinking water from 300 to 8600 m3/h (2 to 55 MGD). The germicidal effect of the UV light inactivates most microoganisms including Cryptosporidium Parvum and Giardia Lamblia.
