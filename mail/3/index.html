Small Standard Ozone Skids

Small Standard Ozone Skids are designed primarily for the treatment of bottled water, but are ideally suited for small drinking water installations. The complete system on a skid, automatically doses the ozone at a given level irrespective of the process water flow rate. The given amount of ozone is introduced to the process water and, after introduction, there is sufficient residence time in order to give the ozone time to work.

TOGC Series

The TOGC Series, including the TOGC8X, 13X & 45X, are corona discharge ozone generators with variable output complete with an integrated oxygen concentrator and compressor. These models are ideal for smaller industrial ozone applications, producing up to 8g/h, 13g/h or 45g/h, respectively.

Each system incorporates function indicators, feed gas flow meter, oxygen concentrator and variable output control, as well as a compressor driven oxygen concentrator with automatically regenerated desiccant columns.
